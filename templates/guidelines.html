Assessment Guidelines

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection.
  • Use a supported browser for the best experience.
  • Disable all notifications to avoid interruptions.
  • Full-screen mode is required during the test.
  • Do not switch tabs during the test.
  • Avoid using the Escape key during the assessment.
  • Keep your device's camera and microphone on if required.
  • Follow all proctoring instructions provided.
  • Do not share your screen during the test.
  • Complete the test within the allotted time.
